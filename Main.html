Orchard Green Restaurant & Lounge

Welcome to Orchard Green, where lounge style tunes fill the air and the kitchen serves up unique takes on meat and seafood. There's a reason Orchard Green has quickly found it's home in New York City.

Delicious Food

Orchard Green takes guests on an ocean-to-table culinary excursion and social dining experience with al fresco dining, a raw bar, exhibition kitchen and 100 year-old olive trees in the famed beach community.

Rustic Atmosphere

Orchard Green features a “Fish Meats Field” coastal cuisine with an emphasis on line-caught seafood and high-quality meats. We delight in pairing the freshest seafood and meat with seasonal produce to create delectable dishes that highlight the flavors of both. With fun twists on classic concepts, we welcome you to enjoy our farm fresh cuisine.

Enjoy!
